Mac Open Web

A collection of open and indie Mac, iOS, and web apps that help promote the open web. Updated May 15, 2019

Submit any new links & issues on this poject at Github

RSS Readers & Discovery

  • Feed Compass

    A Mac app to help you find, preview, and subscribe to blogs

  • Feed Hawk

    Subscribe to a web site’s RSS feed from within Safari

  • NetNewsWire

    A free and open source feed reader for macOS

  • Reeder

    A news reader for the Mac & iOS with support for various sync services

Blogging

  • Feeder

    Create, edit and publish RSS feeds and iTunes Podcasts with Feeder

  • MarsEdit

    Write, preview, and publish to just about anywhere from your Mac

  • Mastodon

    A federated open-source alternative to social networking

  • Micro.blog

    A fast, safe, and independent community of microblogs

Text & Writing

  • Bear

    A beautiful, flexible writing app for crafting notes and prose

  • Byword

    Markdown app for writing in plain text efficiently. Available for Mac, iPhone and iPad

  • iA Writer

    Markdown writing app that removes distractions, giving you a calm, focused writing space

  • Ulysses

    A pleasant, focused writing experience with flexible exporting options

Programming

  • BBEdit

    A text editor that doesn't suck®

  • Coda

    A well-rounded Mac & iOS application for coding websites by our friends at Panic

  • Sublime Text

    A cross-platform, highly extensible text editor

Podcasting

  • Castro

    An iOS podcast app focused on tracking a broad range of shows without getting overwhelmed

  • Downcast

  • Overcast

    A powerful yet simple podcast player for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch

  • Pocket Casts

    A a powerful, easy-to-use app for managing your podcast listening across Android, iOS, and the Web

  • Wavelength

    Record, edit, and publish a microcast (short-form podcast) to your microblog

Read Later & Bookmarking

  • Instapaper

    A no-nonsense bookmarking site for people who value privacy and speed

  • Flickr
