Mac Open Web

A collection of open and indie Mac, iOS, and web apps that help promote the open web.

Updated 14 May 2019

RSS Readers & Discovery

  • Feed Compass — A Mac app to help you find, preview, and subscribe to blogs.
  • Feed Hawk — Subscribe to a web site’s RSS feed from within Safari
  • NetNewsWire — A free and open source feed reader for macOS.
  • Reeder — A news reader for the Mac & iOS with support for various sync services.

Programming

  • BBEdit — A text editor that doesn't suck®
  • Coda — A well-rounded Mac & iOS application for coding websites by our friends at Panic.
  • Sublime Text — A cross-platform, highly extensible text editor

Blogging

  • Feeder — Create, edit and publish RSS feeds and iTunes Podcasts with Feeder.
  • MarsEdit — Write, preview, and publish to just about anywhere from your Mac.
  • Mastodon — A federated open-source alternative to social networking.
  • Micro.blog — A fast, safe, and independent community of microblogs.

Podcast apps

  • Castro — An iOS podcast app focused on tracking a broad range of shows without getting overwhelmed.
  • Downcast — A very cool podcast player for iOS, Mac, Apple Watch, and CarPlay.
  • Overcast — A powerful yet simple podcast player for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.
  • Pocket Casts — A a powerful, easy-to-use app for managing your podcast listening across Android, iOS, and the Web

Read Later & Bookmarking

  • Instapaper — Save anything, read anywhere.
  • Pinboard — A no-nonsense bookmarking site for people who value privacy and speed.

Photo Sharing

  • 500px — A photo sharing community catering to professional photographers.
  • Sunlit — Publish photos to your own blog and discover beautiful photos from other users.
  • Flickr — Photo sharing & organizing, and one of the original photo-based social networks.

Design

  • Acorn — A powerful image editor that feels right at home on a Mac.
  • Hype — ‎Create stunning animated and interactive web content with Hype.
  • Pixelmator — An image editor for Mac and iOS designed to make the most powerful professional image editing tools accessible to everyone.
